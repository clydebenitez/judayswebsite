Reservation Terms and Conditons

The guest will reserve a room by paying a 30% deposit of the room rate or by paying the full amount of the room that the guest is planning to reserve via bank deposit. To confirm that the guest have already made the payment, the guest must take a photo of the deposit slip and and send it via email. The front-desk will first check the calendar and the log book for availability to which then reply to confirm the reservation within 24 hours.

Cancellation Policies

Any guest can cancel their reservation of 7 days prior to reservation. A guest who paid the partial amount of reservation can cancel their reservation but no refund allowed. A guest who paid the full amount of the reservation can cancel their reservation but 50% of the payment will only be refunded.

Property Policies

The Standard Check-Out time is 12:00 noon. Guests who exceeded the check-out time will be charged Php 400.00 for every exceeding 3 hours.

Guests who prefer to extend their stay should inform the front-desk personnel immediately. Extensions are allowed as long as there is no reservation for the room on that day. However, the guest should still pay the full price of their room. If their room are to be occupied for the next guests, they will be transferred in a different vacant room of the same room standards.
