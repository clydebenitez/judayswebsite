WELCOME TO MARTHA'S ROOMS

Martha’s property management is under Goshen Land Property Management Company that assists unit owners and investors in establishing a reliable operation as a property management company. Martha serves property owners by seeking tenants for their units and properties and get a good return on their investments. With the help of Martha, unit/property owners can conveniently earn passive income without the burden and headache of managing their own properties.

North Cambridge Condominiums

North Cambridge units come fully furnished with a 24 hour security embellished with amenities to delight its mainly student, starter families and young professional residents. These amenities include an infinity lap pool, Wi-Fi and wimax areas, an amenity building where commercial stalls will be located, a convenience store center, food court, fitness gym, laundromat, parking slots, cable TV provisions, atrium elevators, sewage treatment plant and a generator set room. Safety features of the condominium buildings have been duly inspected. Goshen Land’s Property Management team maintains the community for the residents’ well-being.
